## ðŸ“ˆ Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: MVP Launch (Months 1-2)

**Goal:** Get first 100 users, validate product-market fit

**Steps:**

1. **Soft Launch - Week 1-2**
   - Launch with Instagram integration only
   - Free tier only
   - Invite 20 beta testers (friends, creator communities)
   - Collect feedback

2. **Product Hunt Launch - Week 3**
   - Create compelling launch page
   - Share on Product Hunt
   - Goal: 500+ upvotes, 50 signups
   - Press coverage from tech blogs

3. **Creator Community Outreach - Week 4-8**
   - Post in creator Facebook groups
   - Share in creator Discord servers
   - TikTok/Instagram content about the tool
   - YouTube video tutorials
   - Reddit (r/NewTubers, r/ContentCreators)

**Success Metrics:**
- 100+ signups
- 50+ active users (made at least 1 match)
- 10+ successful collaborations reported
- NPS score > 50

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 3-6)

**Goal:** Reach 1,000 users, launch paid tiers

**Steps:**

1. **Launch Pro Tier - Month 3**
   - Soft launch to existing users first
   - Offer early bird discount: $19/month (instead of $29)
   - Goal: 5% conversion (50 paying customers)

2. **Content Marketing - Months 3-6**
   - Blog: "How to Find Local Collaborators"
   - Blog: "10 Collaboration Ideas for Creators"
   - YouTube tutorials
   - Case studies from successful matches
   - SEO for "creator collaboration tool"

3. **Influencer Partnerships - Months 4-6**
   - Partner with 5-10 creators (50K-200K followers)
   - They promote the tool to their audience
   - Give them free Pro accounts
   - Affiliate program: 30% recurring commission

4. **Add YouTube Integration - Month 5**
   - Expands addressable market
   - Many creators have both IG + YouTube
   - Announcement drives re-engagement

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000+ total users
- 100+ Pro subscribers ($2,900 MRR)
- 20+ successful collaboration stories
- 40% monthly retention rate

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)

**Goal:** 10,000 users, $30K MRR

2. **Paid Advertising - Months 7-12**
   - Facebook/Instagram ads targeting creators
   - YouTube pre-roll ads
   - TikTok ads
   - Budget: $5K/month
   - Target CAC: $20 (payback in 1 month at $29 pricing)

3. **Partnership with Creator Platforms**
   - Integrate with Patreon, Gumroad, Beacons
